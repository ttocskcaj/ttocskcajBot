___Movement___
Try moving through doors or windows.
```>enter {door_name}```

___Items___
View inventory
```>inventory```
Take Item
```>take {item_name}```
Equip Item (Must be in inventory)
```>equip {item_name}```
Drop an item
```>drop {item_name}```

___Attacking___
Attack creature (with equiped item)
```>attack {creature_name}```

___Stats___
View your stats, such as health etc
```>stats```
